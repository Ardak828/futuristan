About This Portfolio Network

Welcome to my interactive **3D Portfolio Network**! This visualization represents my projects and skills as a cosmic force-directed graph, designed to give you a unique, engaging way to explore my work.

How to Navigate:

  • Explore: Drag and rotate the scene using your mouse or touch.
  • Zoom: Use the scroll wheel or pinch gesture to zoom in and out.
  • Interact: **Double-click (or tap the label)** any floating **node** to open the project details.
  • Drag Nodes: **Click and hold (or tap and hold on mobile)** a node to move it; let go and watch the physics simulation re-establish connections.

This immersive is built using **Three.js** for the 3D rendering (nodes, edges, and the beautiful starfield background) and a custom force-directed simulation to govern the movement and layout of the project nodes.
